Before you sign on...

This app uses WordLand to send posts to WordPress. To do that we need you to authorize that via wordpress.com.

Click the button below to start the process. WordPress.com will ask you to approve. Then you'll come back here, ready to go.

If you've used WordLand before, you may not be asked anything at all.
